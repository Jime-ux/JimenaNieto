ABOUT ME

I'm Jimena, a UX / UI designer with the ability to improve design.
With the experience of working in sales of a product for years, I know the value of developing a desirable product that has business objectives. I learned from scratch to design and I keep learning so that the product has an impact for the user.

In 2019, I transitioned to a career in UX design. I believe that user experience design is at the perfect intersection of creativity and analysis; While there is plenty of room for creative expression, I love that UX design is user-centric and solutions are based on real human needs. In 2018 I decided that I wanted to travel and work what I like, I started the transition to UX / UI design. The two parts have the sense of analyzing and creating to meet a goal, so that solutions are based on real human needs.

He began to study HTML5, CSS3 programming as I went deeper into the subject, I found one of the courses related to figma, that was the beginning of my learning towards UX / UI design.

I spend a lot of time increasing my knowledge of UX and UI concepts and processes, new courses that help me complement what I have learned so far, and my goal is to travel again and be able to work in Europe as a designer.

Outside of design, I am a huge fan of traveling, spending time with my family, friends and telling them about all the trips I was able to do in a short time. If you would like to work together, I would love to hear from you!
